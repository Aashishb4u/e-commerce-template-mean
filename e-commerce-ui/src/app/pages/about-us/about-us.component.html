What we believe

At IBluechem Group India, our dedication revolves around trust, service, and unwavering support for our partners and customers. Our primary objective is to deliver Best-In-Class services and support to meet the demands of this competitive market. Serving as the exclusive supplier and distributor of the URO ONE® Product in India, IBLUECHEM GROUP INDIA is headquartered in Chatrapati Sambhajinagar, Maharashtra, India, and stands out as an innovation leader in industrial lubricants, driven by long-term vision and proactive measures.

Established in 2014, IBLUECHEM GROUP INDIA is an independent and successful, owner-managed company originating from India, boasting international subsidiaries and distributors. Renowned globally, the URO ONE® brand is recognized as one of the strongest names for special lubricants and fuel additives across various applications and industries. As a premium partner to the industry, we specialize in understanding our customers' fluid management needs, earning their trust in URO ONE® as a highly innovative and reliable specialist with extensive expertise in coolants and industrial greases.
